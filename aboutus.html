Get To Know Us!

About Us

Welcome to Nature’s Haven, your ultimate guide to discovering the best outdoor adventures and natural parks! Our mission is to help you find the perfect park or mountain to explore, whether you’re seeking a peaceful retreat or an adrenaline-pumping adventure.

Our Story

Nature’s Haven is more than just a website; it’s a family legacy. Our journey began in the early 1980s when our grandparents, Roosvelt and Laila Lusma, fell in love with the great outdoors. They started a small guide service, helping friends and neighbors discover hidden gems in the wilderness. Their passion for nature and adventure was contagious, and soon, what began as a hobby turned into a thriving family business.

Our Family

For over four decades, the Lusma family has been dedicated to promoting outdoor activities and preserving natural beauty. Each generation has added their unique touch, expanded our services and reaching more nature enthusiasts. Today, we continue to honor our grandparents’ legacy by providing comprehensive guides, tips, and resources for outdoor lovers of all ages.

Our Mission At Nature’s Haven, we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the wonders of nature. Our goal is to make it easy for you to find and enjoy the best parks and mountains, whether you’re planning a weekend getaway or a long-term adventure. We are committed to offering accurate, up-to-date information and inspiring you to explore the great outdoors responsibly.

Join Us

We invite you to join our community of nature lovers. Share your experiences, discover new destinations, and help us continue our family’s tradition of celebrating and preserving the natural world. Together, we can ensure that future generations will also have the chance to enjoy the beauty and adventure that nature has to offer.
